How to be a Host:

  • 1. Put boxes in the location to collect gifts.
  • 2. Acknowledge your community about this event.
  • 3. Wrap the gifts.
  • 4. Put a card on the gift which says"From: Santa To:(benefeciary's name)".
  • 5. Deliver the gift at the time that the benefeciaries request.

    How to be a Benefeciary

  • 1. Look at the google map.
  • 2. Find a host nearby.
  • 3. Submit the forms on the website.
  • 4. Wait for you gifts!

SHHHHH is a nonprofit organization where we deliver gifts to those in need just in time for Christmas. On this website, you will be able to ask for gifts to recieve, and have the option to donate gifts as well. Host companies will recieve gifts from donators, and will then deliver to the families.
